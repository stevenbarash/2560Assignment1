I always enjoy a good cup of coffee.

Here's how I make one.

I always use a cevze to make my coffee.

A cevze is a Turkish coffee pot that you directly pour the grounds and water into in order to make the coffee. You then put the cevze on the stove on medium heat. It's best to use as finely ground coffee as you can get, as it will brew better and you will end up drinking less grounds.

I personally prefer to use Mehmet Effendi Turkish Coffee grounds (pictured below). It has a very unique taste, especially when prepared in a cevze.

You can purchase this coffee and a cevze at the Middle Eastern food store Sultan Bey located on Craig St. and Centre Ave.
